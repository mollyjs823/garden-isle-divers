Private Boat Charters

Our private boat dive charters are a smash hit with visitors to Kaua'i. When you book a private boat dive tour it’s all about you! We tailor the day to your specific interests and experience levels. Instead of getting stuck diving a site that everybody but you wants to see, it’s only you we have to please. Whether it’s scuba diving, snorkeling, or simply enjoying a day on the water, we can deliver one of the most rewarding experiences of your vacation to Kaua'i. Call today or click on the “Book Now!” button above to start the adventure.

Group Boat Charters

Our group boat dive charters never include more than six passengers! That means you will never be ignored or have to struggle to get a chance to get a close look at the turtles or any of the other amazing creatures we are privileged to experience while exploring Kauai underwater.

Private Boat Special

You own the boat for a day! You get to decide who comes and where we go. The crew will include a captain and a licensed instructor who will guide you along the reef ensuring that you are safe, comfortable and having the time of your life. Price is good for two divers each doing two guided dives. Add up to four other friends/family members. Select your adventure below and book now.

Book your dive adventure now!

We offer two ways to book your scuba dive Kaua'i adventure.

  1. One is online and you can do that right here, right now. Just find the trip you want below and click on BOOK NOW.
  2. If you prefer to speak to someone, or have questions, then choose the second method of booking – by phone or email (Contact Us).

Online direct bookings require full payment at the time of booking. If this is a problem then please call or email for alternative payment plans. We will still need credit card information to make a reservation but we can delay payment to allow you to pay by other means (we accept all modern forms of payment including PayPal, Venmo, and of course, cash).

Price includes all gear needed plus drinks and snacks!

**Important Notices**

All dive bookings have a 24 hour cancellation policy. If you fail to cancel or fail to show up your credit card will go without you!

Boat dives are for CERTIFIED DIVERS ONLY! Private charters can include non-diving passengers who may have the opportunity to snorkel or swim.
