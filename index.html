Boat

Garden Isle Divers’ boat dives are the best way for certified divers to see Kaua'i’s premier diving sites. We specialize in underwater fun and adventure! We offer unforgettable private or group boat charters guided by highly trained and experienced instructors.

Shore

We offer shore diving tours that extend individualized attention while we explore the underwater world of Kaua'i's coral reefs. You can choose between private or small group guided shore dives to make the most of your experience. There is no need to be certified!!!

Kaua'i

Kaua'i is famous for its lush beauty but few people get to experience how that incredible scenery extends to the reef that surrounds the island. Whether it’s shore dives or boat dives, when you dive Kaua'i with us, you won't be disappointed.

About

If you are planning a trip to Kaua'i, plan on spending a morning with us scuba diving and exploring the vibrant reef that surrounds this unique and special place. Whether you prefer the casual and relaxing nature of a shore dive or the great possibilities offered by a boat dive, let us get you underwater and show you why we love diving Kaua'i – The Garden Isle.
